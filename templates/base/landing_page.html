Your Gateway to a Connected Campus Community

SDM CampusFlow is designed to bring students and faculty closer, fostering a sense of collaboration and interaction beyond the classroom. Whether it's sharing academic advice, discovering exciting college events, or building communities around shared interests, CampusFlow creates a space for seamless communication. With user authentication based on University Seat Numbers (USN), it ensures that only verified members of the SDM college community can participate, making it a safe and trusted environment for everyone. This project was developed by Sheikh Umaid, a student at SDM College of Engineering and Technology, specializing in backend development. You can view more projects and contact the developer through their portfolio: Sheikh Umaid.

Connect

Built for SDM, CampusFlow allows students to connect with peers, access study resources, and advertise events. With verified profiles, it ensures a secure and engaging campus experience.

Engage

CampusFlow encourages meaningful interactions within SDM's community. Share academic advice, upload media, and explore student initiatives. It’s your portal to connect, learn, and contribute to college life.
